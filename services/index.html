Go-To-Market Strategy and Activation

We work with you to devise a successful Go-To-Market blueprint including a Fortune 1000 market feedback loop, sales enablement and training program, collateral creation and addressable market analysis.

Pitch and prospecting narrative:

  • A clear, powerful and approved story to communicate your brand, purpose and solutions
  • Modular and customizable content for each sector and prospect
  • Both a succinct "elevator pitch" and a longer narrative sales story

Defining Your Addressable Market:

Amplifyer will identify, build, and target a list of relevant prospects for your company’s services. Whether your company’s offerings are niche and vertical-specific or meaningful to all businesses, we ensure that you’re connecting with the right people.

Sales Materials and Training:

  • Customizable pitch decks
  • One-sheets / leave-behinds
  • Case studies
  • Discovery questions/talk track to elicit client feedback and uncover pain points
  • Regular cadence of in-person/virtual team training days

Fortune 1000 Feedback Loop:

Amplifyer has deep connections among Fortune 1000 clients and advertising agencies across North America. We’ll put your products in front of our warm network, garnering constructive feedback and potential partnership opportunities.
